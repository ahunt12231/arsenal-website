Our Team

Arsenal Football Club is an English professional football club based in Islington, London. We play in the Premier League, the top flight of English football. Our club has won 13 league titles (including one unbeaten title), a record 14 FA Cups, two League Cups, 16 FA Community Shields, one European Cup Winners' Cup, and one Inter-Cities Fairs Cup. In terms of trophies won, We are the third-most successful club in English football.

We play at The Emirates Stadium (known as Arsenal Stadium for UEFA competitions) which is a football stadium in the UK. It is our stadium. It has a seated capacity of 60,704, making it the fifth-largest football stadium in England by capacity.

Mikel Arteta

Manager & Pioneer

Mikel Arteta Amatriain (born 26 March 1982) is a Spanish professional football manager and former player, who is the current manager of Premier League club Arsenal. He has the best win percentage of any Arsenal manager.

Martin Ødegaard

Captain & Leader

Martin Ødegaard (born 17 December 1998) is a Norwegian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for and captains both Premier League club Arsenal and the Norway national team.

Aaron Ramsdale

Goalkeeper & Protector

Aaron Christopher Ramsdale (born 14 May 1998) is an English professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Premier League club Arsenal and the England national team.
